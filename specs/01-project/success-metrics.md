# Success Metrics

> **Version:** 2.0.0  
> **Last Updated:** 2026-06-14

## Metrics That Matter

### Traffic Metrics

| Metric | Year 1 Target | Year 2 Target | Year 3 Target |
|--------|---------------|---------------|---------------|
| **Organic Traffic** | 100K/month | 500K/month | 1M/month |
| **Pages per Session** | > 2.0 | > 2.5 | > 3.0 |
| **Average Session Duration** | > 3 minutes | > 4 minutes | > 5 minutes |
| **Bounce Rate** | < 60% | < 55% | < 50% |

### SEO Metrics

| Metric | Year 1 Target | Year 2 Target | Year 3 Target |
|--------|---------------|---------------|---------------|
| **Indexed Pages** | 650+ | 2000+ | 5000+ |
| **Domain Rating (DR)** | 30 | 50 | 60 |
| **Referring Domains** | 50+ | 200+ | 500+ |
| **Core Web Vitals** | All green | All green | All green |

### Conversion Metrics

| Metric | Year 1 Target | Year 2 Target | Year 3 Target |
|--------|---------------|---------------|---------------|
| **Newsletter Signups** | 5% of visitors | 7% of visitors | 10% of visitors |
| **Affiliate CTR** | > 3% of visitors | > 5% of visitors | > 7% of visitors |
| **Calculator Usage** | 10% of visitors | 15% of visitors | 20% of visitors |

### Revenue Metrics

| Metric | Year 1 Target | Year 2 Target | Year 3 Target |
|--------|---------------|---------------|---------------|
| **Monthly Revenue** | ₹5L | ₹50L | ₹1Cr |
| **Revenue per 1000 pageviews** | ₹500 | ₹750 | ₹1000 |
| **Affiliate Partners** | 30 | 100+ | 200+ |

### Content Metrics

| Metric | Year 1 Target | Year 2 Target | Year 3 Target |
|--------|---------------|---------------|---------------|
| **Articles Published** | 250+ | 1000+ | 3000+ |
| **Average Article Length** | 2000+ words | 2500+ words | 3000+ words |
| **Content Update Frequency** | Monthly | Bi-weekly | Weekly |

## Metrics That Do Not Matter

| Metric | Why |
|--------|-----|
| **Pageviews** | Vanity metric without context |
| **Unique Visitors** | Without engagement context |
| **Social Likes** | Do not correlate with revenue |
| **Twitter Followers** | Do not drive traffic or revenue |
| **Page Rank** | Deprecated |
| **Vanity Keywords** | Ranking for irrelevant high-volume keywords |
