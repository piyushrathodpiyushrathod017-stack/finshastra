# Business Model

> **Version:** 2.0.0  
> **Last Updated:** 2026-06-14

## Overview

**Content-first affiliate model.** We educate users first, then recommend products. Revenue follows trust.

## Revenue Streams

| Stream | Description | Phase | Expected Revenue |
|--------|-------------|-------|------------------|
| **Affiliate Commissions** | Bank/NBFC partnerships for loans, credit cards | Phase 1 | 60% of revenue |
| **Google AdSense** | Display advertising on content pages | Phase 1 | 20% of revenue |
| **Sponsored Content** | Clearly labeled paid partnerships | Phase 2 | 10% of revenue |
| **Lead Generation** | Form submissions to partner banks | Phase 2 | 10% of revenue |

## Revenue Does NOT Come From

- Direct lending (no RBI NBFC license)
- Hidden fees
- Selling user data
- Misleading recommendations

## Business Model Canvas

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Value Proposition** | Trustworthy, jargon-free financial guidance for Indian consumers |
| **Customer Segments** | Age 22-45, salared professionals, metro/Tier-1 cities |
| **Channels** | Website, SEO, social media, email newsletter |
| **Customer Relationships** | Educational content, tools, calculators |
| **Revenue Streams** | Affiliate, ads, sponsored, lead gen |
| **Key Resources** | Content, SEO, expert authors, brand trust |
| **Key Activities** | Content creation, SEO optimization, affiliate management |
| **Key Partners** | Banks, NBFCs, financial experts, content writers |
| **Cost Structure** | Content creation, hosting, tools, marketing |

## Unit Economics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)** | ₹0 (organic traffic) |
| **Lifetime Value (LTV)** | ₹50+ per user (affiliate + ads) |
| **LTV:CAC Ratio** | Infinite (organic model) |
| **Revenue per 1000 pageviews** | ₹500-1000 |
| **Affiliate conversion rate** | 3-5% |
| **Newsletter conversion rate** | 5% |
