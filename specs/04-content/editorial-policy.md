# Editorial Policy

> **Version:** 2.0.0  
> **Last Updated:** 2026-06-14

## Our Commitment

FinShastra is committed to providing accurate, unbiased, and helpful financial information to Indian consumers. Our editorial policy ensures the quality and integrity of all content we publish.

## Editorial Standards

### Accuracy

- All financial data is verified against official sources (RBI, CIBIL, bank websites)
- Interest rates, fees, and charges are checked regularly and updated within 7 days of changes
- Statistics are cross-referenced with 2+ sources minimum
- Data currency is always cited (e.g., "As of March 2026, RBI data shows...")

### Independence

- Affiliate partnerships do NOT influence content recommendations
- Sponsored content is clearly labeled and does not affect editorial decisions
- Editorial team has final say on all content decisions
- No advertiser or partner can influence article rankings or recommendations

### Transparency

- Every article with affiliate links has disclosure at top
- Sponsored content is labeled "Sponsored" or "Paid Partnership"
- Author credentials and affiliations are disclosed
- Last updated dates are visible on all content

### Expertise

- All articles are written or reviewed by subject matter experts
- Authors have verifiable credentials (CA, CFA, MBA Finance, etc.)
- Minimum 3 years of relevant experience required
- Expert bios and credentials are displayed on every article

## Content Review Process

```
Writing → Fact-Check → Editorial Review → SEO Review → Publish
```

| Stage | Owner | Requirements |
|-------|-------|--------------|
| Writing | Author | Follow article template, meet word count |
| Fact-Check | Editor | Verify all data against official sources |
| Editorial Review | Editor | Check tone, clarity, accuracy |
| SEO Review | SEO Team | Verify metadata, schema, internal links |
| Publish | Admin | Final approval, schedule publish |

## Corrections Policy

### How to Report Errors

If you find an error in our content, please email corrections@finshastra.in with:

- Article URL
- Description of error
- Source for correction (if available)

### How We Handle Corrections

1. All corrections are reviewed within 48 hours
2. If verified, the article is updated promptly
3. A correction note is added to the article
4. The "Last Updated" date is refreshed
5. Significant corrections are logged in our corrections page

### Correction Note Format

```markdown
---

**Correction:** {date} — This article was updated to correct {description of correction}. The previous information was {incorrect information}. The correct information is {correct information}.

---
```

## Affiliate Disclosure

### What We Disclose

- We earn commissions from partner banks/NBFCs when users apply through our links
- This does not affect our recommendations or rankings
- We only partner with RBI-regulated institutions
- Affiliate links are clearly marked with `rel="sponsored"`

### How We Disclose

Every article with affiliate links includes:

```markdown
*Disclosure: This article may contain affiliate links. When you apply through these links, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. This helps us keep FinShastra free. See our [affiliate disclosure](/affiliate-disclosure/) for details.*
```

## Sponsored Content Policy

### Rules

- Maximum 2 sponsored articles per month
- Must meet same quality standards as editorial
- Must be clearly labeled "Sponsored" or "Paid Partnership"
- FinShastra retains editorial control
- Sponsored content does not affect organic rankings

### Labeling

```markdown
---
type: sponsored
---

# {Title}

*Sponsored Content*

{Content}
```
