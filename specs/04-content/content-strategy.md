## Content Quality Standards

### Must Have

- Expert author with verifiable credentials
- Fact-checked information from official sources
- Clear, jargon-free language
- Internal links to related content
- FAQ section with schema markup
- Last updated date visible
- Affiliate disclosure (if applicable)

### Must NOT Have

- Thin content (< 800 words for articles)
- Duplicate content
- Placeholder or lorem ipsum
- AI spam patterns
- Keyword stuffing
- Misleading claims
- Outdated information

## Content Workflow

```
Ideation → Research → Writing → Review → Edit → Publish → Monitor → Update
```

| Stage | Owner | Duration |
|-------|-------|----------|
| Ideation | Content Team | 1 day |
| Research | Content Team | 2 days |
| Writing | Author | 3-5 days |
| Review | Editor | 1 day |
| Edit | Editor | 1 day |
| Publish | Admin | 1 day |
| Monitor | SEO Team | Ongoing |
| Update | Content Team | Every 90 days |
